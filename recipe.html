Herbed Hasselback Potatoes

A mouthwatering recipe, crispy on the outside, tender on the inside, and packed with flavor. Perfect as a side dish or a show-stealing appetizer

Ingredients

  • 1 large potato
  • 2 tsp butter
  • 2 tsp extra virgin olive oil
  • ¼ tsp rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degree F.
  2. Wash and dry the potatoes. Use a sharp knife to make thin slits – about 1/8 inch apart – in the potatoes. (Don’t cut all the way through. Leave about 1/4 inch at the bottom.)
  3. Place the potatoes on a rimmed baking sheet and drizzel them with 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  4. Bake at 400 degree for 45 minutes.
  5. In a small sausce, combine butter, 1 1/2 tablespoon olive oil, minced garlic, minced rocemary, and remaining salt and pepper. Heat until butter is completely melted and garlic is fragrant.
  6. Remove the potatoes from the oven and slowly pour the butter mixture over them

Cooking Terms

Sauté
Cooking quickly in a small amount of oil.
Garnish
Decorating a dish with herbs or spices.

Panner Butter Masala

Paneer butter Masala is a creamy curry made with paneer, butter, and tomato gravy.

Ingredients

  • Paneer cubes
  • Tomatoes
  • Butter
  • Spices
      Garam masala
      Chilli powder
      Coriander
  • Cream

Instructions

  1. Fry the paneer lightly.
  2. Prepare the tomato gravy.
  3. Add butter and spices.
  4. Mix in paneer cubes.
  5. Garnish with cream and coriander

Cooking Terms

Simmer
Cooking slowly at low temperature.
Puree
Smooth paste made from blending ingredients.
